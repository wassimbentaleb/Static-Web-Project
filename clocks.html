If you’re looking for the best chess clock on the market, look no further than the DGT 3000 Digital Chess Clock. 2. Analog Garde Classic Chess Clock (175$)

This is one of the best analog chess clock for sale on the market in 2021. The German clockwork is housed in a durable wooden frame that will only grow more beautiful with age. The rustic aesthetic is one of the most common reasons chess players choose analog clocks over digital ones. The Analog Garde Classic Chess Clocks have less features, of course, but the simple beauty of their wooden exteriors and large, round clock faces keeps people coming back to them. When not in use, a chess clock like this could easily decorate a bookcase or study, lending a dignified air to any room.
